# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all people, regardless of their background, identity, or experience level. We pledge to:

- **Be inclusive**: Welcome and support people of all backgrounds
- **Be respectful**: Value diverse perspectives and experiences
- **Be kind**: Treat everyone with dignity and respect
- **Be collaborative**: Work together towards common goals
- **Be professional**: Maintain a high standard of conduct

## Expected Behavior

Community members are expected to:

✅ **Do:**
- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Be respectful of differing opinions and experiences
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy towards other community members
- Ask questions before making assumptions
- Give credit where credit is due

❌ **Don't:**
- Use offensive, derogatory, or discriminatory language
- Make personal attacks or harassment
- Post confidential or private information
- Engage in spam or self-promotion
- Create hostile or exclusionary environments
- Discriminate based on any protected characteristic

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct. We take violations seriously and will:

1. **Investigate** reported violations fairly and thoroughly
2. **Document** instances of unacceptable behavior
3. **Communicate** with involved parties
4. **Take action** as appropriate:
   - Private warning or correction
   - Temporary or permanent removal from community

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by:

- **Email**: sdas@live.com
- **Private message**: Via GitHub issues (private note)
- **In person**: Contact a community leader directly

All reports will be handled confidentially.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All community spaces (GitHub, issues, discussions, etc.)
- Official events and gatherings
- Spaces where someone is representing the project or community
- Private communications related to project business
